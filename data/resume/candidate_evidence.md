# Candidate Evidence

This file is used for JD matching evidence, not resume formatting.

The evaluator reads evidence primarily from:

- `## Professional Experience`
- `## Selected Projects`

Keep bullets concrete and defensible. Mention real tools, scale, and outcomes.
